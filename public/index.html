What is wakesurfing

What exactly is wakeSurfing? wakesurfing is a water sport, where you are pulled behind a boat, while riding a board, then you throw the rope back inside the boat and surf the wake created by the boat.

Why I enjoy wakesurfing

As a child I grew up wakeboarding. By the age of 15, the impact caused by the force of jumping on a wakeboard was causing injuries to my lower back. As an alternative, I decided to start wakesurfing. WakeSurfing is far less impact and in my opinion just as fun.

What equipment is needed for wakesurfing

  • A ski boat
  • Tow rope
  • Life Jacket
  • Wakesurf board, surfboard or skim board
